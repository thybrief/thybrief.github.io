It's time to get it rolling...

We were fed up...fed up with our inboxes getting filled with so much information. Someone said 'Software is eating the world', we felt information is almost killing the world.

So what do we do about it? We went on to stick our heads out and found the solution for it, then we pondered upon why just us?

Then we bring you thybrief. Newsletter for the savvy people.

We keep things very simple here, for you we bring the most important and valuable information. You are in for it. We, the curators go through hordes of information and find the best stuff out there. We, be precise and to the point.

If you are an entrepreneur, a guy crazed to having a startup, want to build things or just want to read some really serious stuff. We bet'cha, we will be there for you.

Its just you and us with our plain and simple monologue. Join us in the camaraderie here every week.
